Common questions, straight answers.

How do I forward my calls to Trade Answer

It's simple - you set up a call divert on your mobile to your dedicated Trade Answer number. We send you step-by-step instructions when you sign up, and we're here to help if you get stuck. Most customers have it set up in under two minutes.

Who answers my calls

Every call is answered by your Trade Answer service, powered by advanced voice AI technology - designed to sound natural, warm and professional. Not sure what to expect? Call us and we'll give you a demonstration.

Will my callers know they're not speaking to a human

Most won't notice. Your answering service is designed to handle calls naturally and professionally. If a caller specifically asks, your answering service will be upfront - but in our experience, callers are far more focused on getting their job sorted than on who's answering.

What happens if your answering service can't answer a question

Your Trade Answer service is briefed on your business, but if a caller asks something outside that briefing, the caller is told politely that you'll call them back with the details - and their information is captured for your follow-up.

What happens when I reach my monthly minute limit

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit and invite you to upgrade to a higher plan. We always give you plenty of notice - we won't just cut you off.

Is there a contract or lock-in period

No lock-in contract. Just 30 days written notice to cancel, and you're done. The setup fee is non-refundable, but your monthly subscription can be cancelled at any time with notice.
